# Intro to LAN

## What is a LAN?

A LAN (Local Area Network) is a network that connects devices in a small area, such as a home, school, or office.

## Key Characteristics

- Covers a limited area  
- High speed  
- Usually privately owned  

## Devices in a LAN

- Computers  
- Routers  
- Switches  

## What I Learned

- LAN connects devices in a small area  
- It is fast and efficient  
- Most home networks are LANs
