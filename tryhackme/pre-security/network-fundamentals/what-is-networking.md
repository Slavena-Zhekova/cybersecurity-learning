# What is Networking?

## Definition

Networking is the practice of connecting computers and devices so they can communicate and share resources.

## Why Networking is Important

- Allows devices to communicate  
- Enables internet access  
- Makes data sharing possible  

## Examples

- Accessing a website  
- Sending emails  
- Connecting to Wi-Fi  

## What I Learned

- Networks connect devices together  
- Communication happens through protocols  
- Networking is the foundation of cybersecurity
